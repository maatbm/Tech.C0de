COMPUTER SCIENCE & PROGRAMMING TIPS

An initiative by students at IFSul Sapiranga to promote the technical course in computing and help the academic community.

Who are we?

A group of IFSul Sapiranga students who are here to share knowledge and programming tips! From students for students!

Devs: Emmanuelli Scherer, Ericka Vargas, Jean Goulart, Kauã Lucas, Laura Silva, Luiz Alves, Matheus Moraes e Natália Weyermuller

What are our goals?

Our group, determined to make a difference in the IT area, has as its main goals to promote the sharing of knowledge and create a collaborative environment where everyone can learn and grow together. We develop accessible educational resources to help colleagues overcome academic challenges.
